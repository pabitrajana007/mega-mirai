Our Services

Details about the services we offer.

Real estate property mediation

We help select appropriate properties in consideration of location and structural characteristics. We also strive for reasonable real estate transactions, and have many repeat clients thanks to the know-how we provide through our partnerships with affiliated NPOs.

Office and Store Building Support

Based on the idea that "if the office changes, the people will change, and if the people change, the company will change", we will suggest the perfect work environment for you based on your work style, image, and the parts of your business you want to improve. We provide a one-stop service for everything to do with your office or store environment. What's more, we will put together a dedicated project team for you, and by carrying out design and construction in-house, reduce your operational labor and costs. We have a proven track record of about 10,000 installations, and we are chosen by a great number of customers.
